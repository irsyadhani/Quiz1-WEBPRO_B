Museum Mpu Tantular

The Mpu Tantular Museum is a popular tourist attraction. The area is about 3 hectares and it's a shame to miss it. There are many historical objects ranging from royal relics, geologists to numismatics.

Citra Garden Waterpark

The next tourist spot in Sidoarjo is Citra Garden Waterpark. This recommended tourist attraction has many rides for children. For example, like under water adventure, racer slides to other rides. There are also outbound packages for visitors who want an adrenaline-pumping tour.

Citra Garden Waterpark is located in Citra Garden Housing, Ruko Sentra Niaga Blok RK 32. This location is not far from the city center and can be accessed using public transportation. Some of the facilities provided are parking area, canteen, gazebo and children's games.

Candi Pari

The next tourist spot is Pari Temple. This historical tourist attraction is no less interesting. According to historical records, Pari Temple is part of the pattern of the Khamer Temple in Burma and the Champa Temple in Thailand. This temple was built during the reign of Hayam Wuruk.

Until now, the temple is still used as a place of worship for Hindu residents. Often visited by school children to find out its historical value. The location is in Porong District, Sidoarjo Regency.
