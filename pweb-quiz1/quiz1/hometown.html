About Sidoarjo

Sidoarjo District is a district (kabupaten) of East Java, Indonesia. Sidoarjo Regency is bordered by Surabaya city and Gresik Regency to the north, by Pasuruan Regency to the south, by Mojokerto Regency to the west and by the Madura Strait to the east.


History of Sidoarjo

Djoyohardjo. In 1859, Sidokare had separated with Surabaya and become its own regency. Then in couple month, Sidokare had change become Sidoarjo. Sidoarjo recognized for its sea products like milkfish, prawn, crab, cockle mussel, etc.
