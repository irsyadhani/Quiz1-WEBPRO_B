Bandeng Presto

Bandeng presto is a typical Indonesian food originating from the city of Semarang, Central Java. This food is made from milkfish (Chanos chanos) which is seasoned with garlic, turmeric and salt.

Kue Lumpur

Kue Lumpur is a light snack with the main ingredients of coconut milk, potatoes, wheat flour and eggs. Vanilla is used as a fragrance and is often garnished with raisins and sliced ​​young coconut on the surface. This cake is classified as a wet cake so it does not last long.

Rawon

Rawon is an Indonesian beef soup. Originating from East Java, rawon utilizes the black keluak nut as the main seasoning, which gives a dark color and nutty flavor to the soup

Lontong Kupang

Lontong kupang or kupang lontong is the name of a typical food from East Java. This food is famous especially in the Arekan area of ​​East Java. On the east coast of East Java, the famous lontong kupang is the Kupang Keraton.
